S2. Ans.(b)  
Sol. Information Given in the Question: 
A completes work in 10 days  
B completes in 2X days  
B + C together complete in X days  
A and B work together for 5 days 
C completes the remaining work in 10 days 
We need to find the value of X. 
Concept/Formula Used in the Question: 
Work = Rate × Time 
Detailed Explanation: 
Let the total work work (LCM of 10, 2X, X) = 30X 
A’s rate = 30X/10 = 3X/day 
B = 30X / 2X = 15 units/day 
B + C = 30X / X = 30 units/day 
So C = 30 - 15 = 15 units/day 
Work done by A and B in 5 days = (3X + 15) × 5 
Remaining work = 30X - (3X + 15) × 5 = 30X - (15X + 75) = 15X - 75 
C does remaining in 10 days → 10 × 15 = 150 
So: 15X - 75 = 150 
15X = 225 
X = 15

Marked price of A = 300 × 1.4 = Rs 420 
Selling price = Rs 375 
Discount = 420 - 375 = Rs 45 
Discount % = (45 / 420) × 100 = 10.71% = 11% (approx.)
