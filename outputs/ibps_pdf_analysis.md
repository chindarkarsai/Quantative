## Main Findings

### Prelims Pattern

Prelims papers are strongly practice-oriented and usually follow this structure:

- Data Interpretation sets at the beginning or middle of the quant section
- Simplification / approximation blocks
- Number series or missing-number series
- Arithmetic word problems
- Ratio, percentage, average, profit-loss, time-work, and speed-distance questions

The 2025 Prelims papers especially show DI sets such as table-based and line-graph-style questions, followed by calculation drills.

### Mains Pattern

Mains papers are more layered. The quant sections include:

- Caselet or mixed DI
- Pie/table DI combinations
- Quadratic-equation comparison
- Series-based follow-up questions
- Profit-loss with marked price/discount
- SI/CI comparison
- Time and work with variables
- Ratio, percentage, and investment-style questions

Mains questions often combine two or three concepts in one problem, so the app should generate more multi-step questions for Hard difficulty.
